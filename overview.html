what is disruptive technology?

  • innovates existing norms and replaces products or processes with new possibilities
  • offers specific improvements and superior options using limited resources
  • helps underserved markets by creating opportunities for competitors and customers
  • promotes low-cost, high-quality products

overview of cloud technology

  • Virtual space on the internet for storing software, applications, and files
  • Remedies storage challenges and offers a solution to data management
  • Acts as a digital repository for individuals and businesses
  • Revolutionizes how we access and manage digital resources

02

overview of cloud computing

  • Delivers computing services across the internet.
  • Covers a wide range of services, including servers, storage, databases, and software.
  • Enables faster innovation, flexible resources, and economies of scale.
  • Offers a usage-based payment model for optimal cost efficiency and infrastructure flexibility.

03

global spending on cloud

  • The global expenditure on cloud computing technology is expected to steadily climb.
  • Internation Data Corporation states that global spending on cloud computing has reached $706 billion.
  • The upsurge will have implications on business processes, platforms, services, software, among others (Vailshery, 2023).
  • Gartner envisions that by 2026, 75% of entities will readily embrace this digital transformation (Gartner, 2023).
